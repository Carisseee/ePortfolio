Project Idea

Overview

The technology of virtual reality was first invented in 1957 by Morton Heilig (Bridget Poetker 2019). And in recent years, virtual realities are commercialised in the gaming industry. Though virtual reality exists, as far as I know a ‘live VR’ has not been invented yet. It is a tool that will project an ongoing activity or event to people on the other side of the country / world ( basically in any place at any time ).

Motivation

This project idea was inspired during the outbreak of covid-19 and the prevalence of depression in people of different ages. In my point of view, the pandemic has changed everyone’s lifestyle drastically, especially when travelling and gathering is prohibited. Therefore, some people start to withdraw themself from the world as we are only allowed to stay at home until the lockdown is lifted.

In the statistics shown below, the present global rate of depression has increased significantly to a worrying state (Panchal, Kamai, Cox, Garfield 2021). Actions should be taken by all individual to minimise the increasing percentage of people who suffer from depression as this may lead to suicide.

Besides that, this ‘live virtual reality specs’ can be used for concert and travel purposes too.

Description

The design of this project idea will mainly be a combination and modified version of a virtual reality headset and an Apple's AR spectacle. This ‘live virtual reality’ tool comes in a form of spectacles which is less bulky and heavy compared to the virtual reality headset we saw in the market these days. A 3D effect will be displayed once wearing on the ‘live virtual reality specs’.

This ‘live virtual reality specs’ is compatible with phones and a 360° camera. It can simply be explained by the concept of us enjoying a 360° view of a scenery on our phone but a 3D effect that will be displayed using the ‘live virtual reality specs’. This will be something new and interesting for everyone to experience.

In the midst of this pandemic, many outdoor activities, in particular activities that would cause a huge crowd gathered in an enclosed area are banned. In consequence, many events are forced to call off during this period of time. The ‘live virtual reality specs’ will allow all events to be carried out smoothly at one’s home which will help to avoid the outspread of the virus unconsciously.

As mentioned before, the ‘live virtual reality specs’ can be commonly used for 3D video calls.

A 360° camera can be widely used in more formal or big events such as company meetings, performances, exhibitions, etc..

Many meetings and classes are now carried out online via Zoom, Microsoft teams , etc… People often feel less motivated because of the surrounding environment and ambience. However, with the invention of this ‘live virtual reality specs’,classes and meetings will be more lively as people will feel like they are in the office or lecture hall respectively rather than just sitting and being in front of the screen. They are more likely to engage themselves and can have a “face-to-face” interactions with one another This tool will be helpful in getting people to involve themselves in their daily activities.

Concerts can also be carried out through this ‘live virtual reality specs’. A 360° camera will be required during the concert to enable fans to have a full view of the performance. At this point of time, they will also get to have a chance to be in close contact with their idol virtually. It will definitely be a very immersive, exciting and unforgettable experience.

Apart from that, virtual travelling is made possible through these ‘live virtual reality specs’ too. People will be able to see the world in a more realistic way without being physically there due to the 3D effect that is carried out by the ‘live virtual reality specs’.

The possible obstacle of this project might be the cost of the ‘live virtual reality’. Marketing it at a reasonable price is relatively difficult as the hardware and software required to build this tool is not straightforward and would require a lot of manpower. I would call it success if the price of this tool is affordable for all individuals so everyone will get a chance to own it. Hence, recycled material can be considered in making the exterior of the ‘live virtual reality specs’ to reduce the price of raw material.

Tools and Technologies

Code editors such as Visual Studio Code , Atom , notepad and IntelliJ are the examples of open source tools that are available for everyone which is one of the essential steps that should be taken in the preparation of inventing the ‘live virtual reality specs’ for program writing. The hardwares required to build this ‘live virtual reality specs’ are a processor, an operating system and a USB port.

Skills Required

A good programming and coding skills is certainly required to ensure this tool is functioning and operating well. However, I personally think that Python will be perfect for this project as it is beginner friendly and more straightforward to be written.Besides that, the frame of the spectacles will be made up of recycled material such as plastic, a bit of metal and glass for the lenses. High resolution projector that comes in a miniature size will be perfect for the ‘live virtual reality specs’ to display high quality videos and also to ensure the projector is featherweight so there won’t be a burden for users to wear on.

Outcome

If this project is successful, people are allowed to feel more connected with each other despite the lockdown and restrictions set by the government. This ‘live virtual reality’ will enable people to have a day-to-day conversation as if they are conversing with each other in real life and face to face in spite of being far from each other. Furthermore, this tool will also be able to help people who are abroad either for work or study to overcome homesickness, especially for those international students who are currently living alone in a foreign country.

Meanwhile, Covid-19 cases will be reduced drastically or everyone can be free from Covid-19 since people don’t have to be in close contact to feel connected to each other which also indirectly reduces the spread of virus.

Primarily, I believe with the invention of this ‘live virtual reality specs’, the chances of people suffering from depression will be reduced to its minimum.
